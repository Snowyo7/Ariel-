✨ Ariel's Journey ✨

Ariel was about 3 years old when her mother passed protecting the merfolk’s from exposure. She first defied her father when she was around 13 years old when she slowly introduced music back into the kingdom despite her father’s decree that forbade any music to be played in the kingdom. She unfreezes her father’s heart allowing music to reign once again. The second time she defied her father was when she engaged in illicit contact with the surface world. Along with that she falls in love with a human, sells her voice to the sea witch in exchange for legs and a chance at true love. She marries Prince Eric after about 10 years she has her first child named Melody who was never told of her mermaid heritage to protect her from harm. Melody was just like her in all aspects but instead of wanting to be human she wished to be a mermaid. Her daughter went on an adventure of her own to become a mermaid she helped a sea witch who tricked her into stealing her grandfather’s triton in exchange for fins. Melody saves the sea creatures and her family and makes a suggestion to her parents and grandfather to remove the wall that divided them. Allowing the merfolk’s and humans to live happily together.
