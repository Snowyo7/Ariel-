✨ Ariel ❤ Eric ✨

In this animated film, a strict King Triton forbade his daughter to ever go up to the surface. One night, a terrible storm swept across the sea. Ariel watched as a prince fell off a huge ship. Ariel pulled Prince Eric to shore saving him. Desperate to see Prince Eric again, Ariel agreed to give her voice to the evil sea witch Ursula. Ursula turned Ariel into a human. But must have him fall in love with her or she would belong to the sea witch forever. They beat the odds and fell in love living Happily Ever After.
